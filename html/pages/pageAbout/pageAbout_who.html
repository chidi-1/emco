About us

EmCo has its headquarters in Moscow. In 2015, it opened an office in London. We are working in continental Europe, the United States, and Southeast Asia through the offices of partner companies.

EmCo has implemented more than 150 projects since its inception. Among our clients have been the largest Russian state,private companies, and a number of intternational corporations.

EmCo is among the pioneers in consulting for the CSR and sustainable development sectors in the Russian market.

Since 2005, EmCo has been a GRI (Global Responsible Care Initiative) member and Organizational Stakeholder (a leading international organization in standardization of reporting).

Оur visoon of CSR

Anna-Maria Ryklina. President, Managing Partner

In today’s world, living conditions and business environment change daily. New challenges appear, as well as new threats and new chances. This renders corporations’ success based on financial accomplishments alone impossible. Business sustainability has gained prominence. That is why integrating the principles of corporate social responsibility(CSR)in the companies’ day-to-day life has been among the EmCo priorities for more than 10 years.

The EmCo mission in CSR is to disseminate ideas of sustainable development, implement advanced global trends, and spread experience in corporate practice.

The EmCo work is based on the principle of active involvement in building a global model of a responsible approach to business, popularization, and promotion of key ideas of sustainable development.

EmCo regards the idea of sustainable development as a concept, exerting a direct practical mpact, here and now, upon all aspects relating to the work of the company (by reducing economic costs, environmental risks, achieving social consensus and the human capital.
